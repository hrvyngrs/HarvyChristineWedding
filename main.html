The Story

From USJ-R to Forever

The Beginning of Us

Harvy and Christine attended the same college, and while they spent plenty of time chatting on Messenger, they never quite had the chance to meet in person. Harvy was extremely shy and usually preferred spending his time at the computer shop, which made face‑to‑face meet‑ups even harder to arrange.

November 2016 - marked the long‑awaited moment when, after countless attempts, they finally met at Jollibee in Gaisano Mactan. Christine took the initiative and made the plan happen, while Harvy arrived feeling a mix of excitement and obvious nervousness.

Christine came straight from school that day, carrying a large shoulder bag. Noticing it, Harvy couldn’t resist breaking the tension with a joke asking if she was already planning to move in since it looked like she had packed all her belongings with her. That playful remark sparked laughter, instantly easing their nerves and breaking the ice.

Even as they enjoyed each other’s company, Harvy kept asking if they would meet again. His nerves got the best of him and feels worried that their first meeting might also be their last, afraid the moment might never happen again.

Little did he know that day wasn’t an ending at all - it was only the beginning. What started as a simple meet‑up blossomed into a beautiful journey filled with laughter, adventures, and a shared love for travel. They now look forward to continuing that journey together as they step into a new chapter of their lives as husband and wife.

Since November 2016, Harvy patiently courted Christine, expressing his love through small gestures and heartfelt moments that made her feel truly special.

At the USJR campus - the very place where their story began and where they first caught glimpses of each other. Harvy prepared a heartfelt surprise: 101 reasons why he loved her, carefully written and placed inside a jar.

That simple yet deeply meaningful gesture marked the start of a beautiful journey together. From that day forward, their bond only grew stronger, eventually leading them to the wedding day they now joyfully share with family and friends.

The Proposal

Harvy had long been dreaming of the perfect moment to ask Christine to marry him. After careful thought and planning, he chose the breathtaking island of Boracay as the setting for this once‑in‑a‑lifetime question. To make everything even more meaningful, he sought the help of Christine’s sister to ask for their parents’ blessing and ensure that every detail went smoothly.

What appeared to be a simple family getaway was, in truth, a beautifully planned surprise. Harvy made sure that both families (including his own) were part of the trip, cleverly using it as the perfect cover for what was about to unfold.

On November 9, 2024, the magic came to life at Puka Beach, with the sun casting its warm glow over the white sand and gentle waves rolling in around them. Surrounded by their loved ones and the natural beauty of the moment, Harvy asked Christine to spend the rest of her life with him.

It was a moment filled with love, laughter, and unforgettable memories, the beginning of a brand‑new chapter in their story and the start of forever.
