Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to build our website?

Depending on the package selected and how quickly you can provide us with your photos and event details, we typically deliver the first draft within 7 to 14 business days.

Can we use our own custom domain name?

Yes! Custom domains (e.g., www.johnandjane.com) are available as an A La Carte add-on. Otherwise, you will receive a clean, customized sub-link on our secure hosting platform.

What happens to the website after the event?

Each package comes with a set amount of hosting time (3, 6, or 12 months). After this period, the website will be taken offline. You can always choose to extend your hosting for a small yearly fee to keep your memories alive.

Do I need technical skills to use the VIP Dashboard?

Not at all! We built the VIP Admin Dashboard specifically to be user-friendly. Managing your guest list and dragging-and-dropping tables on the floor plan builder is as easy as using a smartphone app.
